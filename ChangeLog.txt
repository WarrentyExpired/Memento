- Weapons and Weapon Specials
    Adrenaline System - Weapon special have been removed and replaced with the Adrenaline system. Each weapon class (Swords, Fencing, Marksmanship, etc) has 3 different moves. A general damage move, a defensive move, and a finisher move. In oder to use the moves you need "Adrenaline". Adrenaline is aquired with each successful hit of your weapon up to 100%. Adrenaline also decays over time. The Focus skill lowers the "cost" to use each ability and also how much Adrenaline you loss to decay.
    Innate Abilities - Each weapon class a unique ability that has a base 10% change to proc. Arms lore can increase     the to 25% at 120 skill level. There are 3 new settings in Settings.cs PlayersUseInnates, PetsUseInnates, and MonstersUseInnates.
        Swords - Bleed
        Fencing - Pierce
        Bludgoening - PowerfullBlow
        Fistfighting - Drain Punch
        Marksmanship - Hinder
